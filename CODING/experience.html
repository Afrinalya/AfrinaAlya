(EXPERIENCE)

-This is my experience working as a dropship and agent for Ice Cream Ice Bars-



I started working as an agent and dropship of these Ice Bars on September 19, 2021. This is because, at that time these Ice Bars were still new and well received. So, the demand is very much and in every state there is a lack of agents or dropship. And then, I decided to sell it and at that point, I did not work after SPM. At first, it was a bit challenging too because in my place, many others do not know this brand. Sometimes, they confuse the products that I sell either it food or Ice Cream. So, I took the initiative to create social media to promote this sale of mine. I chose Instagram social media because it is still popular among people including teenagers, children, the elderly, and all types of society. All the information about Ice Bars I put on the site. If there are any questions related to it, I will answer immediately and entice them to buy it.





There are five types of Ice Bars flavors namely Strawberry, Grape, Mango, Passion Pineapple, and Lychee Peach. All this is only RM10 and has 6 packets per box. From promoting it to sending it to the customers, I made it all myself. It was a very interesting work experience. This is because, we only work from home and need to be good at communicating with customers. I was also able to learn how to manage customer frustration, how to organize bookings, use commission money well, and more. Delivery to customers' homes also needs to be on time so that they believe that the business is genuine and not fraudulent. It was something of a very fun work experience that I have ever done in this lifetime and this taught me to be self-reliant.
