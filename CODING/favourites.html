(FAVOURITES AUTHORS)

1. Teme Abdullah

Teme uses the nickname given by his friends while in high school as the name of the questioner to this day. Abdullah's name was added by the author himself behind the name Teme since he first wrote the book in 2016. The way Teme is pronounced is the same as the way the word 'friend' is pronounced in the Kelantan dialect. 'Friend' can be used as a first person pronoun that means 'I'. So, in a nutsheel, the combination of Teme & Abdullah means 'I am only a servant of Allah'.

2. Hilal Asyraf

Muhammad Hilal Asyraf bin Abd Razak or the name of the questioner Hilal Asyraf was born on January 16, 1989. He is a Malaysian writer who actively writes works with Islamic elements. Hilal is also active as an independent speaker, conducting Islamic camps and motivational workshops. He was also invited to promote his motivational book on the Nasi Lemak Kopi O TV show. He founded the Divine Sky website in 2010 and starting September 2016, he managed his own publishing house under the name Hilal Asyraf Rresources.
